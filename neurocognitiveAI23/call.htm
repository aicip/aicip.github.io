    • Call for Papers

    We invite submissions of 4-6 pages (references not included) of original papers on the methodology that discovers learning dynamics from human learning and incorporates it into AI practices. Example topics include (but are not limited to):

    • Experimental and computational methods for discovering learning dynamics at the neuron, neural circuits, neural population, and the behavior levels
    • Examinations of learning dynamics at multiple time scales and their interrelationships
    • Improved methods for decoding dynamics in brain signals
    • How learning dynamics support task generalization (meta-learning)
    • How learning dynamics support causality
    • Incorporation of learning dynamics in reinforcement learning
    • Examinations of the development of learning dynamics in infants and children
    • Training dynamics in continual/lifelong learning settings
    • Network optimization through training dynamics
    • Neurocognitive-inspired AI hardware design
